About me

I am a physician and a researcher in the field of digital pathology. I am interested in the application of artificial intelligence (AI) in pathology, histological image analysis and medicine in general.

I graduated as a physician from Goethe-University Frankfurt am Main in 2024. My medical doctoral thesis, supervised by Prof. Peter J. Wild and Prof. Jochen Triesch, was conducted in cooperation with the Dr. Senckenberg Institute for Pathology (SIP) and the Frankfurt Institute for Advanced Studies (FIAS). It addresses the prediction and characterization of molecular subtypes of muscle-invasive urothelial carcinoma of the bladder using deep learning (it is in the final stages and still needs to be defended).
